Hot Pepper and Onion Pizza

Author: kuyta Hahi
Recipe type: Snacks & Finger Food

Ingredients:

  1. Flour, for surface
  2. 1 lb. pizza dough, at room temperature
  3. Cornmeal, for baking sheet
  4. 1/2 oz. extra-sharp Cheddar, coarsely grated
  5. 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  6. 1 small red pepper, sliced
  7. 1 small yellow pepper, sliced
  8. 1 poblano pepper, halved and thinly sliced
  9. 1 small jalapeño, halved and thinly sliced
  10. 1 tbsp. olive oil
  11. Kosher salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 500°F (if you can’t heat the oven this high without broiling, heat to 475°F).
  2. On a lightly floured surface, shape the pizza dough into a 14-in. oval. Place on a cornmeal-dusted or parchment-lined baking sheet. Sprinkle Cheddar over top.
  3. In a large bowl, toss onion and peppers with olive oil and 1/4 tsp each salt and pepper. Scatter over dough and bake until the crust is golden brown, 10 to 12 minutes.
