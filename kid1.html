Slow-Cooker Chicken-Tortilla Soup

Author:BY MARIAN COOPER CAIRNS NOV 3, 2016
Recipe type: Kid Friendly Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 1/4 lb. skinless, bone-in chicken thighs
  2. 1 small onion, chopped
  3. 1/2 red bell pepper, chopped
  4. 1 garlic clove, chopped
  5. 2 c. chicken stock
  6. 1 (14.5-oz.) can diced tomatoes, drained
  7. 1 (8-oz.) can tomato sauce
  8. 1 (4-oz.) can chopped green chiles
  9. 1 tsp. chili powder
  10. 1 tsp. dried oregano
  11. 3/4 tsp. ground cumin
  12. Kosher salt
  13. Freshly ground black pepper
  14. 2 yellow squash, halved and sliced
  15. 3 oz. green beans, halved
  16. 1 tbsp. fresh lime juice
  17. 2 1/2 tbsp. chopped fresh cilantro, plus more for serving
  18. Sliced jalapeños, sour cream, and tortilla chips, for serving

Instructions:

  1. Combine chicken, onion, bell pepper, garlic, stock, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, chiles, chili powder, oregano, and cumin in a 4-quart slow cooker. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook, covered, until chicken is cooked through on low 7 to 8 hours or on high 3 to 4 hours. Add squash and green beans and cook, covered, for 30 minutes. Remove chicken, discard bones and shred meat; return to slow cooker. Stir in lime juice and cilantro.
  3. Serve topped with cilantro, jalapeños, and sour cream, with tortilla chips alongside.

PER SERVING:

protein: 26 g; fat: 8 g; carbohydrate: 18 g; fiber: 6 g; sodium: 707 mg; cholesterol: 108 mg; calories: 244.
