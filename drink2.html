Mason Jar Citrus Coolers

Author:Kul Kaha
Recipe type:Drinks

Ingredients:

  1. 1 1/2 c. fresh orange juice
  2. 1/2 c. lime juice
  3. 1/2 c. lemon juice
  4. 1 1/2 tsp. sugar
  5. Lemon slices, for serving
  6. Lime slices, for serving
  7. 6 c. club soda

Instructions:

  1. In a measuring cup or pitcher, stir together fresh orange juice, lime juice, and lemon juice. Spoon sugar into each of 8 mason jars and top with juice mixture.
