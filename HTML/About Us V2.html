Our Story

Food Capital founders of 5 persons first met as business students. And
while they discovered a shared passion for great food, they
approached creating system from completely different angles. From
there, they brought the world of cooking to a new platform and
generated the first video-based, user-friendly, and design-oriented
cooking app.

At the beginning of 2020, they launched Food Capital in Google Play
Store. The emotional of videos and high-quality step-by-step photos
struck a chord with their growing user base, who soon realized that
the app's slogan, "anyone can cook".



« Our vision is to shape the digital food space with leading technology and content. »
