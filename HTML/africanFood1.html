Harissa: North African spice paste

Author: Kinchi Karg
Recipe type: African Foods

Harissa is a versatile spicy North African paste, a fiery flavourful condiment perfect for meat, seafood, soup or salad.


Ingredients:

  1. 1/4 cup (18g) dried chilli flakes
  2. 5 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
  3. 2 tbs sweet paprika
  4. 2 tsp coriander seeds1 tsp ground coriander
  5. 1 tsp ground coriander

Instructions:

  1. Place chilli and add 1/4 cup (60ml) boiling water in a small bowl. Stand for 2 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, place remaining ingredients in a mortar and pestle with 2 tsp sea salt and pound until a coarse paste. Add chilli and soaking liquid and mix until combined.
