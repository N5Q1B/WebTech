Herbes de Provence Shrimp with Basil and Pea Couscous

Author:BY ERIKA DUGAN AND KATE MERKER
Recipe type:Eaasy Recipes

Ingredients:

  1. 1 c. couscous
  2. 3/4 c. frozen peas, thawed
  3. 1/4 c. fresh basil, chopped
  4. 1 tsp. lemon zest, plus 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  5. 1 tsp. lemon zest, plus 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  6. Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper
  7. 1 lb. large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  8. 2 tsp. fresh thyme
  9. 1 tsp. fresh rosemary, chopped
  10. 1/2 tsp. dried lavender
  11. 1 tbsp. fresh tarragon, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Cook couscous per package directions. Fold in peas, basil, lemon zest, and 1 tablespoon oil. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Combine shrimp, thyme, marjoram, rosemary, lavender, and 1 tablespoon oil in a bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add shrimp and cook, turning once, until opaque throughout, 2 to 3 minutes. Remove from heat and add lemon juice and tarragon; toss to combine. Serve with couscous alongside.
