1) Peppermint Shake

Author: Nur Aisha
Recipe type:Drinks
2 1/2 c. vanilla bean ice cream, softened

Ingredients:

  1. 2 1/2 c. vanilla bean ice cream, softened
  2. 1/4 c. milk
  3. 3/4 tsp. pure peppermint extract
  4. 3 drops green gel food coloring
  5. Whipped cream and green sanding sugar, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, puree ice cream, milk, peppermint extract, and food coloring to combine.
  2. If desired, top shake with whipped cream and sprinkle with sanding sugar.
