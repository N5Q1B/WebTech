Hot and sour prawn soup

Author: Balkis Haider
Recipe type:Entree

Ingredients:

  1. 250g rice stick noodles
  2. 1.5L (6 cups) chicken stock
  3. 1-2 tablespoons green curry paste to taste
  4. 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  5. 1 tablespoon caster sugar
  6. Juice of 1 lime
  7. 2 tomatoes, chopped
  8. 2 celery stalks, sliced
  9. 20 green prawns, peeled (tails intact), deveined
  10. Sliced red chilli, bean sprouts, coriander and mint leaves, to serve

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice stick noodles in boiling water according to packet instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Place chicken stock, green curry paste to taste, soy sauce, sugar and lime juice in a large pan and bring to the boil. Add tomatoes and celery stalks. Reduce heat to medium-low. Simmer for 5 minutes until tomatoes have softened slightly. Add prawns, then cook for 2-3 minutes until just cooked through.
