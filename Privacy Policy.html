We use so-called “cookies” on our website, wich are small text files used to simplify and accelerate the control of your visit, or might be necessary to enable you to use and access the secure areas of our website.

Depending on where a cookie comes from, so-called first-party cookies and third-party cookies can be distinguished:
First-party-cookies Cookies that are set and stored locally by the controller himself or one of his commissionned subprocessors. Only the operator of the website has an access to these cookies.
Third-party-cookies Cookies that are generated, set and retrieved by third-party providers who do not act as commissionned subprocessors on behalf of the website operator

Depending on the duration of the cookies, a distinction can be made between short-time and persistent cookies:
Transient cookies Cookies, which are automatically deleted when you close the browser. These include in particular the session cookies.
Persistent cookies Cookies that remain stored on your device for a specified period of time after closing the browser.
